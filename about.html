About the system

Local visibility repair, not ranking theater.

The LoCo Code was created for small businesses that need public-facing basics repaired before they can benefit from heavier marketing.

The Problem

Many local businesses lose calls because their public presence is fragmented: old numbers, missing pages, unclear services, neglected profiles, weak trust signals, and contact paths that do not work on mobile.

The Method

The LoCo Code starts with an owner-authorized visibility audit, then turns the findings into a practical repair plan: profile accuracy, landing page clarity, service-area language, forms, calls, and maintenance.

Publishing Structure

The book is published by Signal Tower Press. The direct field kit, Companion Field Course, audit service, setup packages, and managed presence plans are fulfilled through RonDashStella.com.

The operating line stays simple: Amazon sells the book. RonDashStella.com sells the system. Signal Tower Press gives the project authority.
